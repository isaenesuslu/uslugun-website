Pistols

GLOCK

The Glock is an Austrian semi-automatic pistol. The gun was developed for the Austrian Armed Forces in the early 1980s and its production started in 1982. It has become the company's most profitable product line, despite the market's unfounded concerns about durability and reliability, and the ability to be undetected in airport metal detectors.

Knifes

BUTTERFLY

A butterfly knife, also known as a balisong, fan knife or Batangas knife, is a type of folding pocketknife that originated in the Philippines. Its distinct features are two handles counter-rotating around the tang such that, when closed, the blade is concealed within grooves in the handles.

BAYONET

A bayonet (from French baïonnette) is a knife, dagger, sword, or spike-shaped weapon designed to fit on the end of the muzzle of a rifle, carbine, musket or similar firearm, allowing it to be used as a spear-like weapon. From the early 17th to the early 20th century, it was a weapon for infantry attacks.

KARAMBIT

The karambit or kerambit (as used in Indonesian), kurambik or karambiak (both from the Minangkabau language) is a small Indonesian curved knife resembling a claw from the Minangkabau people of West Sumatra. The karambit is one of the weapons commonly used in pencak silat and Filipino martial arts.

HUNTER

A hunting knife is a knife used during hunting for preparing the game to be used as food: skinning the animal and cutting up the meat. It is different from the hunting dagger which was traditionally used to kill wild game.
